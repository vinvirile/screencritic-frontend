The Super Mario Bros. Movie

The Super Mario Bros. Movie is about brothers Mario and Luigi who are transported from Brooklyn to a fantastical world via a Warp Pipe. Mario lands in the Mushroom Kingdom, ruled by Princess Peach, while Luigi ends up in the Dark Lands under the evil King Bowser's control. Bowser plans to marry Peach. With the help of allies, including Toad and the Kongs, Mario and Luigi manage to thwart Bowser's plans.
